# 🏥 સ્વાસ્થ્ય મિત્ર AI (Swasthya Mitra AI)
> **Intelligent Rural Health Triage Platform**

સ્વાસ્થ્ય મિત્ર AI is a comprehensive, AI-powered healthcare platform designed specifically for Primary Health Centers (PHCs) and Community Health Centers (CHCs) in Gujarat, India. It leverages artificial intelligence, voice recognition, and real-time analytics to automate triage, prioritize emergency cases, and assist rural healthcare workers in clinical decision-making.

---

## 🌟 Strategic Challenge Objectives & Winning Edge
Instead of being a simple symptom checker, **સ્વાસ્થ્ય મિત્ર AI** has been explicitly engineered to demonstrate:

* **AI-powered patient triage** with dynamic questioning.
* **Priority-based patient queue** so doctors see critical patients first.
* **Explainable AI** showing exactly *why* a patient is flagged as high risk.
* **Voice-first interaction** solving accessibility for rural and low-literacy users.
* **Offline capability** for low-connectivity Primary Health Centers.
* **Professional dashboards** tailored for doctors, nurses, and administrators.
* **Scalable architecture** suitable for massive deployment across Gujarat's public healthcare network.

> **Note:** These functionalities stay strictly aligned with the challenge's core objective: supporting and augmenting doctors in rural, low-bandwidth clinics using AI, rather than attempting to replace human clinical decision-making.

## 🚀 Complete Functional Modules

### 1. Secure Authentication & Access Control
- Role-based Login (Admin, Doctor, Nurse, Receptionist)
- JWT Authentication & Session Management
- OTP & Password Login, with Password Reset functionality

### 2. Patient Registration System
- New Patient Registration & Existing Patient Search
- QR Code Patient ID and optional Aadhaar integration
- Demographic Information, Medical History, Family History, Allergy Records, and Emergency Contacts

### 3. AI Health Assessment Engine
- AI Symptom Interview with Dynamic Follow-up Questions
- Voice & Text Input with Multilingual Conversation support
- Symptom Timeline Analysis & Severity Assessment

### 4. Vital Signs Monitoring Module
- Blood Pressure, Heart Rate, SpO₂, Temperature, Respiratory Rate, Blood Sugar, Height & Weight
- Automatic BMI Calculation & Pain Score logging

### 5. AI Clinical Risk Engine
- Generates an AI Risk Score (0–100) and priority classification (🟢 Low, 🟡 Moderate, 🟠 High, 🔴 Emergency)
- Explainable AI Recommendations, Clinical Summary Generation, and immediate Emergency Detection

### 6. Role-Specific Dashboards
- **Doctor Dashboard:** Live Patient Queue, AI Clinical Summary, Patient Timeline, Medical History, Vital Trends, AI Recommendations, Clinical Notes, Prescription Management, and Follow-up Scheduling.
- **Nurse Dashboard:** Register Patients, Record Vitals, Upload Reports, Start AI Assessment, track Vaccination Records.
- **Reception Dashboard:** Patient Registration, Appointment Management, Token Generation, and Queue Management.
- **Admin Dashboard:** User, Hospital, Doctor, PHC, and District Management. AI Logs, System Configuration, and Global Analytics.

### 7. AI Voice Assistant Module
- Gujarati, Hindi, and English Voice Support
- Real-time Speech-to-Text & Text-to-Speech with Automatic Language Detection

### 8. Localization & Multilingual Support
- English, ગુજરાતી (Gujarati), हिन्दी (Hindi)
- One-click Language Switching, Localized Reports, and AI Responses

### 9. Emergency Alert & Response System
- Red Alert for Critical Cases & Emergency Notifications to Doctors
- Emergency Queue, Referral Recommendations, and Emergency Case Tracking

### 10. Referral Management System
- AI Referral Suggestion (PHC → CHC → District Hospital)
- Automated Referral Letter (PDF generation) & Referral History tracking

### 11. Medical Reports & Export
- AI Assessment Report, Vital Report, Patient Summary, and Doctor Notes
- PDF Export & Print Support for physical filing

### 12. Patient Health Timeline
- Previous Visits, Previous Symptoms, Previous Vitals, Previous Prescriptions, and AI Risk History

### 13. Health Analytics Dashboard
- Daily Patient Count, Disease Trends, High-Risk Cases
- Village-wise & District-wise Analytics with interactive Charts & Graphs

### 14. Offline-First Capability
- Offline Patient Registration & Offline Vital Recording
- Local Data Storage with Automatic Synchronization

### 15. Automated Notification System
- Appointment Reminders, Follow-up Alerts, and SMS & WhatsApp Ready integration

### 16. Security & Compliance Standard
- Role-Based Access Control, Data Encryption, and Secure APIs
- Complete Audit Logs & Activity History tracking

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS (v4), Vite, Framer Motion
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **AI/ML:** OpenAI/Gemini Integrations (Simulated), Web Speech API for Voice

2. `cd client && npm install`
3. `npm run dev` to start the frontend application
